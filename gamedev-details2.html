the game has been built upon a tutorial by chriscourses at youtube. I have added the stars, the player movements, the booster and the waves. Every object in the game is designed in blender (3D) and then were rendered 2.5D. an sprite sheet were made out of 3D animation. the background layers are downloaded freely from https://bevouliin.com/ .

  • Summary: The player should avoid hitting the enemies and the stars. The enemies will follow the player around the map, but the stars just respawn randomely and moves with different speeds and might leave the map. Player can shoot the enemies with right click and gain points. the player can also use Q to generate a wave which can eleminate all the present enemies, or use "space" to move twice faster.
  • Objective: Getting the highest point.
  • Rules:
    1. If the player hits an enemy or a star, the game is over!
    2. Player only has 3waves, and if he/she uses all, he will not gain extra!
    3. As the time goes by the enemies respawn faster!
    4. The booster recharge after some amounth of time!
